TOURIST DRIVES NÜRBURGRING

Nürburgring Tourist Drives Days 2025

During public sessions on the Nürburgring Nordschleife, also known as tourist days, you have the incredible opportunity to drive your own car or rent one and experience the world's most famous race track.

The race track is open for public use from mid-March to mid-November. Tourist Drives Days at the Nürburgring are available Monday through Friday from 5:00 PM to 7:30 PM, and on weekends from 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM.

Here you can find the schedule of Nürburgring Tourist Drives Days and current prices for the 2025 season.

Collectors and fans flock to the ring

The public sessions in 2025 provide a unique chance to test and refine your driving skills on the legendary Nürburgring Nordschleife. During these sessions, the track has the legal status of a public road, which means that normal road rules apply. It is a time when motorsports fans, collectors, and enthusiasts gather to connect with one another and witness a fascinating display of the latest car models, rare classic cars, and professional drivers setting impressive lap times on the Nürburgring.

Join us on a tourist day and immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere where fellow enthusiasts come together to share their passion for motorsport, appreciate stunning vehicles, and witness the thrilling performances of skilled drivers.

Things to remember

  • Valid driving license: It is essential to possess a valid driving license
  • Traffic Law: Same traffic rules as on other public roads
  • Safety rules: Ensure to read and adhere to all safety rules and regulations
  • No racing license required: Participation in tourist trips does not necessitate a racing license
  • End of tourist drives days: The racetrack will be closed 15 minutes prior to the conclusion of the tourist drives days
  • Weather and accidents: In case of accidents or weather conditions, the track may be temporarily closed

ABOUT US

The Big Garage Nürburgring Project was brought into existence by a team of racing enthusiasts, who couldn’t imagine their lifes without an extreme race track like Nürburgring Nordschleife. Big Garage.de is intended for all those heavily into racing, motorsports and auto tuning. Our project shall help racing rookies and fans discover this insane and thrilling world of adrenaline, drive and hot asphalt charged with tire squealing.

We offer hot taxi laps, sportscars for rental and driving instruction on the most challenging and extreme racing track in the world. Our instructors will help you to get basic driving skills in extreme conditions. Take our driving course and feel confident both on public roads and in a racing car at the speed over 300 km/h, when every turn and braking require your immediate reaction and control of the situation.

We offer you our sports cars, amazing road trips, driving experience, participation in racing series and more. The Nurburgring — is the center for all motorsport fans. Take an unforgettable trip to the most famous race track of the world with us.
